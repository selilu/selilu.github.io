International Programs in Engineering
Study Abroad Website

Overview

The goal of this website is to create a responsive and easy-to-navigate platform to house study abroad resources and informaation for interested engineering students. This website serves as an information hub with advisor contacts, student testimonies, and highlights of available study abroad opportunities. Built with a target audience of students and their parents, the website is formal, professional, and includes lots of quotes and experiences of other students.

Role: Lead developer in charge of meeting with client, planning content updates around important application deadlines, executing redesign of homepage based on design guidelines, and maintenance of site. Developed in an Agile environment.

Key Features

  • Responsive Design
    Optimized for desktop and mobile based on analytics that revealed these were the most commonly used screen sizes for users.
  • Technologies Used
    Built with Divi Web Builder on Wordpress, CSS, HTML, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager
  • Study Abroad Programs Search
    Filterable study abroad programs by term, program type, and country.
  • Visual Appeal
    Colorful images and theme of featuring different countries and landscapes to encourage users to positively view study abroad experiences. Hover effects on buttons to engage users.
